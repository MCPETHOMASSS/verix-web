我們的願景 Our Vision

在這個資訊爆炸的時代，我們的注意力被螢幕不斷分散。VERIX 致力於改變這一現狀，透過「去視覺化」的導航體驗，讓人們重新連接真實世界。 In this era of information overload, our attention is constantly scattered by screens. VERIX is committed to changing this by providing a "de-visualized" navigation experience that reconnects people with the real world.

我們相信，最好的技術應該是無形的，它服務於你，而不是佔據你的注意力。我們的穿戴式裝置透過精密的觸覺反饋，為你提供直覺的方向指引，讓導航變得自然而無意識。 We believe that the best technology should be invisible—it serves you rather than demanding your attention. Our wearable device provides intuitive directional guidance through precise tactile feedback, making navigation natural and unconscious.

加入我們，一起探索科技與人性的新平衡，讓每一次出行都成為一次與世界的真實互動。 Join us in exploring a new balance between technology and humanity, making every journey a genuine interaction with the world.

核心特點 Core Features

Quiet Tech

科技應該安靜地服務，而非喧賓奪主。我們追求低調、高效的解決方案，讓技術融入生活而不打擾生活。 Technology should serve quietly, not demand attention. We pursue低调, efficient solutions that integrate into life without disturbing it.

極簡主義 Minimalism

少即是多。我們去除多餘，保留本質，讓體驗回歸純粹。每一個設計決策都經過深思熟慮，確保功能與美學的完美平衡。 Less is more. We remove the unnecessary, retain the essential, and return to pure experience. Every design decision is carefully considered to ensure perfect balance between function and aesthetics.

可信賴 Trustworthy

我們將用戶安全置於首位，確保每一次體驗都可靠、精準且安心。我們的技術經過嚴格測試，在各種環境下都能穩定運行。 We put user safety first, ensuring every experience is reliable, precise, and secure. Our technology undergoes rigorous testing to ensure stable operation in all environments.

我們開發了專屬的「觸覺語言」，透過精細的振動模式傳達方向與路徑資訊，讓導航變得直覺而自然。 We've developed a proprietary "Tactile Language" that conveys direction and path information through subtle vibration patterns, making navigation intuitive and natural.

低干擾 Low Interference

日常融合 Daily Integration

我們的設備設計時尚、輕便，易於融入日常生活。無論是通勤、徒步還是探索新城市，VERIX 都能成為你的得力助手。 Our device is designed to be stylish, lightweight, and easy to integrate into daily life. Whether commuting, hiking, or exploring a new city, VERIX can be your trusted assistant.

我們的方法 Our Approach

1

感測 Sense

我們的穿戴式裝置透過先進的感測技術，實時捕捉你的位置與運動狀態，為導航提供精準數據。 Our wearable device captures your position and movement in real-time through advanced sensing technology, providing precise data for navigation.

2

理解動作 Understand Movement

邊緣運算技術讓裝置即時處理數據，理解你的步行模式與環境互動，提供個性化的導航體驗。 Edge computing technology allows the device to process data in real-time, understanding your walking patterns and environmental interactions to provide personalized navigation.

3

觸覺語言 Tactile Language

我們開發了專屬的「觸覺語言」，透過精細的振動模式傳達方向與路徑資訊，讓導航變得直覺而自然。 We've developed a proprietary "Tactile Language" that conveys direction and path information through subtle vibration patterns, making navigation intuitive and natural.

4

路徑節奏 Path Rhythm

系統根據你的步行節奏調整提示時機，讓導航體驗自然流暢，猶如本能反應，無需刻意思考。 The system adjusts the timing of prompts according to your walking rhythm, making the navigation experience natural and fluid, like an instinctive response that requires no conscious thought.
